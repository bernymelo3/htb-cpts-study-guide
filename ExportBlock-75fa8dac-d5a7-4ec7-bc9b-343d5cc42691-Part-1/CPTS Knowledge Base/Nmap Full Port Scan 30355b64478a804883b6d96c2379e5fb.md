# Nmap Full Port Scan

Category: Enumeration
Command: nmap -p- --min-rate=1000 -T4 <target_ip>
Description: Performs a comprehensive scan of all 65535 TCP ports with increased speed. Use this as your initial scan to discover all open ports before running detailed service enumeration. The --min-rate flag ensures packets are sent quickly, and -T4 sets aggressive timing.
Tags: enumeration, nmap
