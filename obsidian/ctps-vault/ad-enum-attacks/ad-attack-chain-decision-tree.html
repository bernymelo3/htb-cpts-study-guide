Q9 ▶
What we need
Enumerate who has dangerous permissions (GenericAll, WriteDACL, etc.) over the Domain Admins group
Attempt 1 — PowerView on MS01 (likely fails)

✗ PowerView not recognized / AMSI blocks it
AMSI silently kills the import. The module loads with no error but no functions are available. Don't waste time with AMSI bypasses on HTB — they're unreliable. Move to Linux tools.

Evil-WinRM's Bypass-4MSI crashes the session. Manual bypasses produce null errors. PSExec shell mangles PowerShell IEX. Don't go down this rabbit hole.
